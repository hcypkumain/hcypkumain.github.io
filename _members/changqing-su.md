<span data-i18n="Changqing Su_1">Su Changqing is currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Peking University, advised by Professor Yu Zhaofei. He obtained his bachelor's degree in Automation from Xiangtan University, and later was recommended for admission to the Master's program in Computer Science at Shandong University. His research interests focus on ultra-fast 3D imaging and brain-inspired vision. He has participated in a number of research projects, achieving fruitful results in fields including light field microscopy, event cameras, and spike cameras. As co-first author, co-corresponding author, and in other roles, he has published numerous papers in journals and conferences such as Photonics Research, Optics Express, CVPR, and ACM MM. Additionally, he has participated in the authorization of multiple patents.</span>
